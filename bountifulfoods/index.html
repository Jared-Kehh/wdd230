Summary Of Carlsbad

LEGOLAND

Legoland California Resort is a theme park, miniature park, and aquarium located in Carlsbad, California, based on the Lego toy brand. Excitement in building and constructing your own dreams. It is made for all ages!

Learn more

The Flower Fields

The Flower Fields is a flower garden in Carlsbad Ranch in Carlsbad, California. It is open to visitors between March 1 and Mother's Day. It has vibrant colorful flowers that will make you fall in love.

Learn more

Sea Life Aquarium

Family-friendly exhibits featuring more than 5,000 sea creatures, plus a play area & group packages. Packed wonderful information about the sea. It is a great way to explore Carlsbad and brings joy to your vacation!
